Parnian Shabani Kamran

I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, at University of California, Davis.

I work under the supervision of Prof. Caleb Stanford and am a member of the DavisPL lab where I care about the reliability and verifiability of systems especially code language models—ensuring they deliver outcomes that align flawlessly with their intended purpose. I am also previlidged to work under the guidence of Prof. Prem Devanbu during my PhD at UC Davis.

Research

My research focuses on enhancing the trustworthiness of code language models through formal methods and by applying the latest prompting and reasoning techniques to improve their reliability and performance in code-related tasks.

Programming languages, Formal Verification, LLMs, Security, Fuzz Testing (Fuzzing)
