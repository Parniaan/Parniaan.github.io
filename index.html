Parnian Shabani Kamran

I am a PhD student at the University of California, Davis. I work under the supervision of Prof. Caleb Stanford. I am also previlidged to work under the guidence of Prof. Prem Devanbu during my PhD at UC Davis. I work on the development of safe, reliable, and scalable AIware, i.e., AI-powered software, by combining formal verification methods with Large Language Models (LLMs) and Code Large Language Models (Code LLMs). My research focuses on enhancing the safety and reliability of LLM Agents in the context of software development and code-related tasks.
